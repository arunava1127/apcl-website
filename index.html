About

The Automotive Propulsion Control Laboratory (APCL) aims at transforming the energy storage, conversion and power transfer processes for automobiles and other mobile applications with mechatronic systems and advanced control methodologies.

Common Rail Fuel Injection System

Compared to traditional fuel metering systems such as carburetors, direct fuel injection provides more flexible and precise fuel supply. This helps engineers design engines with low emissions without compromising power output.

Transmission
